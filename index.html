We distinguish ourselves from other service providers in the market by combining our expertise in the different area of tutoring in web technology to deliver sound background in programming to your students. We are group of academy based in Lagos and Portharcourt with expertise in tutoring students in web design, web development; and other value added services.

Our mission for going into business is to build an organization that excels in all its activities. To build young students that are well grounded in programming at a young age
